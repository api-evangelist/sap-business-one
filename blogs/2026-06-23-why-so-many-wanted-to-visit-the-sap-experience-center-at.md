The SAP Experience Center brought the “new SAP” to life at SAP Sapphire. It guided visitors through the story of a major sporting event—from early planning to game day—and ended with a special souvenir. Andreas Wendel is very pleased with the outcome: a total of 11,200 visitors explored the SAP Experience Center at SAP Sapphire Orlando and Madrid .
